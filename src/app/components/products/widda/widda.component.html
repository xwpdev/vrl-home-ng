Widda

Widda is a VR Archery Game crafted mainly for the Oculus Rift. Widda in English means “The Shooter”

In this game, players are opportune to combine their techniques, intelligence, and strategy at its best for a fierce and intense clash against the Troll King and his army. You are required to build towers to help protect your castle and also put your bow and arrow techniques to use against the waves of the Troll King and his army. Enemies come like waves, so depending on the player’s skill, the player can score points and be able to move forward. If you think you have what it takes to protect your kingdom, then come along and test your skills in Widda on fully functional VR devices.

We also intend to release this game on Steam and Oculus store as well as do custom modifications based on any client’s various desires. If a company desires to promote its event with the Widda VR game. We can modify the game according to their requirements and transform it to be used as marketing material in their event.
